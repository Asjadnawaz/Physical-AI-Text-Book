## Getting Started

The chatbot is integrated directly into the textbook website. You'll see a chat icon in the bottom-right corner of every page. Click it to open the chat interface.

### Opening the Chat Interface
1. Navigate to any page in the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics textbook
2. Look for the chat icon (ðŸ’¬) in the bottom-right corner
3. Click the icon to open the chat interface
4. You can now ask questions about the textbook content

## Basic Usage

### Asking Questions
- Type your question in the input field at the bottom of the chat interface
- Press Enter or click the Send button to submit your question
- The chatbot will search the textbook and provide a response based on the content
- Responses typically appear within 5 seconds

### Example Questions
- "What is Physical AI?"
- "Explain embodied cognition theory"
- "What are the challenges of humanoid locomotion?"
- "How does the humanoid form enable cognitive transfer?"

## Text Selection Feature

One of the powerful features of this chatbot is the ability to ask questions about specific text on the page.

### How to Use Text Selection
1. Select text on the webpage by clicking and dragging over the text you're interested in
2. The selected text will appear in the chat input area with a preview
3. Type your question about the selected text
4. The chatbot will prioritize information related to your selected text in its response

### Example Use Cases
- "Explain this concept more deeply" (after selecting a complex concept)
- "What does this mean in simpler terms?" (after selecting technical content)
- "Give me an example of this" (after selecting a theoretical concept)

## Understanding Responses

### Response Structure
Each response from the chatbot includes:
- **Content**: The answer to your question based on textbook content
- **Sources**: Links to the textbook sections that informed the response
- **Confidence Score**: A measure of how confident the chatbot is in its response (0-1 scale)

### Citations
When the chatbot provides information, it will often cite specific sections of the textbook. Look for references to chapters, sections, or page numbers that you can explore for more detail.

### Response Limitations
The chatbot only uses information from the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics textbook. It cannot access external websites or general knowledge outside the textbook content.

## Tips for Best Results

### Formulating Questions
- Be specific with your questions for better results
- Ask about concepts you see on the current page
- Use text selection for contextual questions
- If you don't get the answer you expected, try rephrasing

### Using Context
- The chatbot remembers the context of your conversation
- You can refer to previous questions or answers in follow-up questions
- Starting a new session will clear the conversation history

### Getting More Information
- If a response seems brief, ask "Tell me more about this" or "Can you elaborate?"
- Use text selection to drill down into specific parts of the textbook content

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### "I couldn't find relevant content..."
- This means the chatbot couldn't find information in the textbook related to your question
- Try rephrasing your question
- Make sure your question is related to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics topics
- Use more specific keywords from the textbook

#### Slow Responses
- Initial queries may take longer as the system loads
- Subsequent queries should be faster
- If consistently slow, check your internet connection

#### Unexpected Responses
- The chatbot is limited to textbook content only
- Responses are generated based on semantic search of the textbook
- If something seems incorrect, verify with the textbook content directly

### Getting Help
If you encounter issues not covered here:
1. Clear your browser cache and reload the page
2. Try the same question again
3. Contact the textbook support team with details of the issue

## Advanced Features

### Session Management
- Each chat session is tied to the page you're viewing
- Your conversation history is maintained during your session
- Sessions are stored in your browser's local storage

### Privacy
- Conversations are stored locally in your browser
- No personal data is collected or transmitted
- Your questions help improve the system while maintaining privacy
